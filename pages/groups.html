Videogame Dev

Our goal for the Video Game Design Group is to work together and create a game while exploring the various aspects of game development. To achieve this, we divide our section into 3 groups: the game designers, script writers, and asset creators. Through the different groups available, students have the opportunity to explore all aspects of game development and choose one that they like the most.

Cybersecurity

Welcome to Computer Club. My name is Carson Krahe and I am the leader of the CyberSecurity Group. So, what do we do here? We do CyberPatriot, which is a nationwide competition where we find vulnerabilities in virtual computer systems. Now that sounds like a lot, but it is basically a scavenger hunt to find ways to make your system more secure. So, some questions you may have: Do I need any experience? No, you don’t need any experience. I help you up to the first competition and you have teammates to help you. What will you be learning about? We learn how to write scripts, the basic vulnerabilities that systems have, how to handle a CyberPatriot competition, and other cybersecurity topics. So come out to cybersecurity and we will be glad to have you. I hope to see you there.
